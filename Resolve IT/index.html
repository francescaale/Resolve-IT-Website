About Us

RESOLVE IT : Computer Repair & IT Support

Resolve I.T is a student-led PC repair learning company set up by the Digital, Business & Professional department.

Resolve I.T provides an opportunity for students to learn new skills and develop their employability attributes in a work like environment. It helps students develop transferable skills enabling them to work with others both professional, internal and external clients, meeting set deadlines and working in aspects of IT that they haven't previously had a chance to experience.

The company has students involved from both Computer Science and Business curricular areas that make up their two departments Hardware and Business. Their business team involves marketing, accounting, administration and human resources whereas their hardware team mainly consists of IT Technicians who are the main view of the company.
The company has a full staffing structure with student managers overseeing all projects and business managers and accounting overseeing the administration and finances of the company.

Resolve I.T works with a wide-variety of clients both internal and external to the college, such as the Create Department, #WeMakeICTPossible, the NHS, the college’s IT department and more. The learning company works with these clients to provide refurbished computers to disadvantaged children, perform professional diagnosis on faulty laptops and develop software solutions such as websites and design.
